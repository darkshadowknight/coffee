Founded in Cainta, Rizal, BrewSha is a proudly Filipino café that celebrates local flavors, creativity, and cultural heritage. More than just a coffee shop, BrewSha builds a sense of community through curated events, handcrafted beverages, and a commitment to Filipino craftsmanship.

Founded in Cainta, Rizal, BrewSha is a proudly Filipino café that celebrates local flavors, creativity, and cultural heritage. More than just a coffee shop, BrewSha builds a sense of community through curated events, handcrafted beverages, and a commitment to Filipino craftsmanship.

A platform for meaningful brewing and Filipino Craft

We built Brewsha to celebrate and uplift Filipino craft through small-batch, ethical brews. By partnering with social enterprises and local artisans, we aim to create both delicious coffee and sustainable impact. Our partners include Kalsada Coffee PH, Hope in a Bottle, and other homegrown changemakers.
